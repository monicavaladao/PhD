Detailed Description

A blind kriging surrogate model.

Papers:

  • "Blind Kriging: A New Method for Developing Metamodels", V.R. Joseph and Y. Hung and A. Sudjianto, ASME Journal of Mechanical Design, 2008
  • "Functionally Induced Priors for the Analysis of Experiments", V.R. Joseph and J.D. Delaney, Technometrics, 2007

Limitations:

  • quartic is the highest order supported
